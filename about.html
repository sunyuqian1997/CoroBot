  • CoroBot
    A virtual virus chatbot trained on COVID- 19 related tweets and social media posts. This project aims to provide a way to communicate with unfamiliar creature, and relect on our own existence. This project aims to provide a way to communicate with the illusioinal, unfamiliar creature and reflect on our own.
  • About
    Author
    Yuqian Sun, artist, interaction designer and computational artist. Her works range from generative animation to interactive installation and AI practices, especially chatbots. She showed her works in exhibitions including The Media Facade in Yekaterinburg in Russia, 2020 Artificial life conference art show and Renovators OPPO 2020 campus global artists projects. Her research works about narrative intelligence have been presented at conferences like ACM womENcourage 2020.
